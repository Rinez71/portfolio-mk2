Persona Aloy

Aloy

Technology

  • Devices: Primarily uses Dell Inspiron 5000 running Windows 10 and Samsung Note20 running Android 11
  • Proficiency: Proficient with technology. Aloy is very comfortable behind a keyboard and enjoys using her mobile outside of work
  • Passion for technology: Aloy enjoys the advantages technology grants her and tries her best to stay up-to-date with new technologies

Attitudes and Needs

  • Aloy is using my site to gain information about me as a person/employee, gauge my development skills by viewing gallery of projects, and contact me for a project
  • Aloy needs a website revamped for her insurance/brokerage firm
  • Aloy is using my site because it allows her to gain a plethora of information about me in a short amount of time at one place

Frustrations

  • Aloy finds it frustrating that she is not usually able to see developer's projects before speaking with them
  • Aloy is also frustrated that he cannot gauge what kind of person he is hoping to hire for a project

Role

  • Power User

User Story

As a power user, I would like to view credentials and personality of a potential hire.

Use Case

Aloy has recently has recently started a business and would like a place on the web to allow users to order her BBQ.

  • PreCondition: Aloy accesses portfolio website
  • PostCondition: Aloy has now used the order form to hire Robert Inez to build her site.

Interaction Flow

  • Dib clicks on 'Sign Up' option
  • AlienGram displays form with 'Email', 'Username', 'Password', and 'Confirm Password'options
  • Dib fills in required information
  • AlienGram prompts Dib to prove he is not a robot using a reCaptcha
  • Dib completes reCaptcha
  • AlienGram confirms he is at least not a robot...Redirects to home page
  • Dib clicks on 'Post!' button in the top right of his screen
  • AlienGram displays opens a form with various fields(Date/Time, City/State, Duration and Description) for him to fill out
  • Dib fills out the required information and looks for a way to attach his images. He clicks on the paperclip icon in bottom of form
  • AlienGram displays an area for user to attach images(jpeg, png, etc)
  • Dib uploads images of his interaction
  • AlienGram displays a 'Are you sure?' box with the options 'Yes' & 'No'
  • Dib clicks on 'Yes' in order to confirm his post
  • AlienGram posts Dib's post to the feed of other reports
